What are cookies?

Cookies are a set of data that a server deposits in the user’s browser to collect registration information Internet standard and behavioral information visitors to a website. That is, they are small files of text that is stored on your computer’s hard drive and that serve to identify the user when he reconnects to the website. Its purpose is to record the user's visit and save certain information. Its use is common and frequent on the web already which allows the pages to run more efficiently and get more customization and analysis on the user behavior.
